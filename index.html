About PhonePe

PhonePe is a financial technology company headquartered in Bangalore, India. It was founded in December 2015.

PhonePe provides an online payment system based on Unified Payments Interface (UPI), which is a new process in electronic funds transfer launched by National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI).

It is licensed by the Reserve Bank of India for issuance and operation of a Semi Closed Prepaid Payment system.
